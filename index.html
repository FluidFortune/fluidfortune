[ ABOUT FLUID FORTUNE ]

Fluid Fortune is an independent forge dedicated to building local-first software. We operate under the Clark Beddows Protocol: all compute stays on your machine, you own your data, and there are no gatekeepers.

We build tools for the era of private intelligence. If it requires a subscription or an API key, we don't make it.
